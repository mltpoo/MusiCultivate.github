◄ CHORDOPHONES ►

Chordophones are a category of musical instruments that produce sound by vibrating strings. These instruments typically consist of strings stretched across a resonating body or soundboard, which amplifies the vibrations and projects the sound. The strings can be plucked, bowed, or struck to produce musical tones.

examples of chordophones ►

Guitar

The Guitar is played by plucking or strumming the strings. It is a wooden instrument that has a hole on its body to produce sound and is used in many different genres of music.

Violin

The Violin is played by using bow to vibrates on its strings. It consist four string and it's mostly well-known instrument in classical music.

Harp

The Harp is one of the oldest musical instrument. It designed with vertical frame with strings that are plucked to produce sound.

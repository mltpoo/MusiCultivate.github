◄ KEYBOARDS ►

Keyboard instruments are a category of musical instruments that produce sound by pressing keys on a keyboard. The keys are connected to mechanical or electronic mechanisms that cause strings, air columns, or other sound-producing elements to vibrate, generating musical tones.

examples of keyboards ►

Piano

The Piano is played by using fingers to press keys. it has pedals under of it, used by a foot to sustain the sound of the instrument and it has 66 - 88 with 7 octaves keys.

Organ

An Organ produces sound by means of pipes or reeds and is operated by a keyboard or other means. It is a complex instrument that has been used for centuries in various forms and sizes.

Synthesizer

A Synthesizer is an electronic musical instrument that generates and manipulates sound electronically. It is designed to create a wide variety of sounds, including imitating traditional acoustic instruments, generating unique synthetic tones, and producing complex sound effects.

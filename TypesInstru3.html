◄ PERCUSSIONS ►

Percussion instruments are a category of musical instruments that produce sound by being struck, shaken, or scraped. They typically consist of a resonating body or surface, which can be made of various materials such as wood, metal, or skin, that vibrates when struck. The vibrations create audible sound waves and produce distinct rhythmic patterns.

examples of percussions ►

Drums

The Drums are played by striking them with sticks or hands. Drums is a set of different parts, It consist tomtoms, snare, kick or bass drum and also cymbals.

Cajon

The Cajon is a wooden box that is played by striking the front or top surface with the hands, fingers, or various implements.

Tambourine

The Tambourine is consisting of a circular frame with metal jingles or discs attached to it. It is played by shaking, striking, or tapping the instrument to produce sound.
